Search syntax

Terms are matched against entry title and description. Separate terms with spaces or explicit operators.

Syntax Behaviour
term1 term2 OR — entries matching either term
term1 AND term2 AND — entries matching both terms
term1 XOR term2 XOR — entries matching one but not both terms
"dark matter" Quotes group words into a single phrase

Examples

neutrino muon — either word

neutrino AND muon — both words

"dark matter" AND tau — phrase and word

upgrade XOR maintenance — one or the other, not both

alpha AND beta OR gamma — evaluated left to right
